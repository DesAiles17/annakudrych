I love people. Although it's a cliche, I genuinely do feel that way. It is genuinely amazing how different people can be from one another, and I feel as like it is my unspoken goal to get to know them all. Or at the absolute least, try to include as many of them as you can into my life.

My job is enhanced by my warmth and cheerfulness, which makes me particularly good when working with families. Getting the most delirious giggles out of children during a photo shoot is one of the finest parts of my profession since I love kids so much. My ability to establish a cordial connection with my customers comes easily to me, and as a result, I'm quite excellent at calming them down and getting them to smile for the camera. Since many of us detest getting our photos taken—after all, we're just human—having a photographer who makes you feel comfortable is a truly wonderful thing. This specific skill is also useful for my business work.
